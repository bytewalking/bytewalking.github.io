The journey is the reward

关于我

你好，我是 Evan。

一名在代码、城市与自我之间不断往返的旅人。

我是软件工程师，常年在系统、数据、架构之间钻洞打洞， 也在游戏行业里摸过 Web3 的风，追过链上的浪。 我的工作让逻辑保持锋利，但生活不止于逻辑。

我喜欢 AAA 游戏的沉浸感，也喜欢猫咪蜷在脚边那份温柔的确定。 我会在凌晨的公路上找回自由，也会在一本书或一部电影里找到新的入口。 摄影是我最近的新冒险： 我带着富士的 XT5，试着把城市的秩序与混沌一起按进快门里， 捕捉那些人群里停顿的空隙、桥下被风吹弯的光。

过去几年我经历了漫长的关系拉扯也经历了放下， 经历了团队变动、重构压力、职业怀疑， 也经历了重新建立自我边界、理解心绪的过程。 这些故事不必被浪漫化，但它们确实把我雕刻成现在的样子。

我正在学习语言，学习心理学，学习成为更清晰的自己。 未来希望成为一个能在世界各处落脚写字、拍照、工作的数字游民。

这个博客，是我给自己的一条回声走廊。 记录我在技术、游戏、旅行、成长里的所思所感。 有时坦白、有时锋利、有时带点倔强的幽默。 我想这里会慢慢变成一张地图， 把过去、现在和未来的我连成一条线。

如果你愿意，也欢迎你一起来走走。

About Me

Hi, I’m Evan — a software engineer, a gamer, a wanderer between cities, and someone still learning how to live with clarity.

I build systems, chase down bugs, shape architectures, and currently work in the world of Web3 gaming.
My work keeps my logic sharp, but life stretches far beyond logic alone.

I love the immersion of AAA games and the quiet company of cats.
I enjoy late-night drives, and the way a good book or film can open a hidden door in the mind.
Photography is my newest obsession — with a Fuji XT5 in hand, I try to capture the balance between motion and stillness in the city,
those fleeting pockets of order in an otherwise restless world.

In the past few years, I’ve walked through long relationships, breakups, attempts at repair, and the eventual release.
I’ve dealt with team reshuffles, technical rebuilds, and career doubts.
None of these moments were glamorous, but they shaped me in ways I couldn’t ignore.

Now I’m learning languages, psychology, design — slowly assembling a clearer version of myself.
Someday, I hope to become a digital nomad, carrying my work and curiosity across borders.

This blog is where I store the echoes:
notes on technology, games, travel, self-reflection, and the small sparks that appear along the way.
Sometimes honest, sometimes sharp, occasionally touched with a quick drop of humor.
I want this place to grow into a map — one that connects who I was, who I am, and who I’m becoming.

If you’ve wandered in, feel free to walk with me for a while.

Software Developer, Programming Enthusiast, Game Player and Life Adventurer
